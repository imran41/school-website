Our Vision

To be a beacon of academic excellence in Darbhanga, Bihar — nurturing students who are not only intellectually strong but also compassionate, disciplined, and socially responsible individuals ready to face the challenges of tomorrow.

Admission Process
2025–26

We welcome students from Nursery to Class 10. The admission process is simple, transparent, and student-friendly.

01
📋

Fill Application

Collect or download the admission form and fill in all required student and parent details carefully.

02
📁

Submit Documents

Submit required documents: Birth certificate, previous report card, Aadhar card, and 4 passport photos.

03
🗣️

Interaction & Assessment

A friendly interaction session with the child and parents to understand the student's learning needs.

04
✅

Confirmation & Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete fee payment and receive the welcome kit, books list, and uniform details.
